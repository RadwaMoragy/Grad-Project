About Our Project

Welcome! We are a group of passionate friends and recent graduates who developed this platform as our capstone graduation project.

This website is a demonstration of our work: a cutting-edge vulnerability scanner that harnesses the power of machine learning. Our goal was to create an intelligent, automated tool that can effectively identify, analyze, and report potential security flaws in web applications.

This project was born from our shared fascination with cybersecurity and a desire to build something meaningful. We're excited to share our work and contribute to making the web a safer place.
